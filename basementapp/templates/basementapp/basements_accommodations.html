Steps to Find Accommodation:

Step 1: Click on the Get Started button or Accommodation above. This will take you to the accommodation page



Step 2: On the accommodation page, look for the room availability indicator . If the letter "Y" is displayed on the indicator, it means there is a vacant room/s, else If the letter "N" is displayed on the indicator, it means there is NO vacancy "All rooms occupied".



Step 3: Click on the info button to see more details about the private accommodation.



NOTICE: Basements can not reserve a room for you, therefore you are advised to meet or secure the room with the land lord outside basements as soon as possible.
